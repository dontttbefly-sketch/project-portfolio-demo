Our Approach

We design for the way pets actually live.

Most pet goods are made for stores, not homes — bright, branded, and stacked on a shelf. We start the other way around: from a kitchen counter, a worn-in armchair, the linen sheet your dog has chosen as his own.

Every piece in The Daily is meant to be left out. To be folded into the rhythm of a household, not stowed in a closet between visits.

— The studio, Brooklyn
