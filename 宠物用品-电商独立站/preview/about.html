Our Story

Quiet goods for the everyday.

Nest & Den began in a Brooklyn studio in 2023 with a single observation: there is a meaningful difference between things that are made for pets and things that are made for the homes pets live in.

We started Nest & Den because we couldn't find a single pet bed we wanted to keep in our living room. Our cat had taken up residence on a folded linen throw; our dog had claimed a corner of an old wool rug. The pet store options — bright, branded, plastic — felt like a different kind of object entirely.

So we made the bed we wanted: linen on the outside, recycled fiber on the inside, sewn in a small workshop in North Carolina. We added a bowl, a leash, a coat. We tested everything in 200 households across the country before we sold a single one.

What we learned: the pieces that last are the ones designed for the slow rhythm of home — washed often, slept on, and folded away only when company is the kind that doesn't like animals.

Today Nest & Den is a small team in Brooklyn working with a network of family-owned mills and workshops across the U.S., Italy, and Lithuania. We restock a single tight collection — The Daily — instead of chasing seasonal drops. We're trying to make less, better, and to do it for a long time.
