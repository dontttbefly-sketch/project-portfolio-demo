Made for cats & dogs

Outer shell: 100% European flax linen, OEKO-TEX® Standard 100 certified. Inner cushion: 100% post-consumer recycled PET fiber, sourced from U.S. plastic bottles. All thread is 100% recycled polyester. Sewn in North Carolina.

Remove inner cushion. Machine-wash cover cold, gentle cycle. Tumble-dry low or line-dry. Avoid bleach. The linen will soften and develop a subtle patina with each wash.

Small: 24" × 18" × 4". Suited to cats and dogs up to 25 lbs.
Medium: 32" × 24" × 5". 25–55 lbs.
Large: 40" × 30" × 6". 55–95 lbs.

How it's made

Stitched in North Carolina, washed before it ships.

Each Daily Bed is cut from a single roll of flax-linen and sewn by a small workshop in High Point, North Carolina. We pre-wash every cover so the piece you receive feels as soft on day one as it will on day three hundred.
